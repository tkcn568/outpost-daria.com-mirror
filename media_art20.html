In the Media

Seventeen
March 1999

The March 1999 issue of Seventeen magazine had a full-page advertisement for the premiere of Daria's third season. Daria is, of course, as enthusiastic as ever... "Hooray. I'm so excited." The caption reads: "America's most sarcastic teenager returns. Wednesdays at 10pm ET." Note that this advertisement also appeared (in various forms) in other magazines at the time, including TV Guide and teen-oriented publications like Teen People.
